My beverage of choice is Root Beer. It's flavor profile stands out to me amongst other carbonated drinks - an intense sweetness accompanied by a subtle spice, not something too common in other sodas. When chilled, makes for the perfect refreshment on a warm, sunny day. Better yet, it proves itself as complement to countless culinary combinations - a great among them, is the timeless Root Beer Float. An enhanced experience for those with a strong sweet tooth, the classic blend of root beer and vanilla ice cream is a true staple of sweet treats.
