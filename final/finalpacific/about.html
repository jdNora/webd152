Our Story & Mission

In the late 1920s, our founder Fred Marlow built a cabin home for himself along the coastline, where it still stands to this day. He was enamored by the sights and sounds of nature, and wanted nothing more than to share the experience with others.

With the rapid urban expansion taking place in Southern California, he knew that the beautiful landscapes that he wanted to call home would eventually be overtaken. Combining forces with local conservationist efforts, countless of acres of land would be protected as a state park.

The Pacific Trails Resort was established in 1953, and the Marlow family has made it their mission to help preserve the beauty of the coastal park and provide an experience to those looking for an authentic getaway in nature. The resort staff has expanded to over 150 employees, all dedicated to bringing guests with true relaxation.

The Resort has served over 4 million guests since opening its doors, treating visitors to fun experiences and lasting memories - with activities like kayaking, guided hikes, yoga, and so much more!
